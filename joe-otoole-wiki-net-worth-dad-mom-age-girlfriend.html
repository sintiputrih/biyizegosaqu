Joe O’Toole On Families Of The Mafia

The second season of Families of the Mafia premiered on 15 July 2021 with a “Chapter 1 – Reflection” and concluded on 9 September 2021 with a “Chapter 10 – Conclusion”.

Two new families joined the Gravanos, O’Tooles, and LaRoccas this season in Staten Island.

The series goes deeper into the unfinished business lurking in the mafia families. And so you must not miss to find out how Joe O’Toole fits into one.

Joe has been on the show for both seasons. The first series of Families of the Mafia had aired from April to May in 2020.

If you already do not know, Families of the Mafia is a retooling of the brief-lived Made in Staten Island.

Joe O’Toole Dad, Mom

The O’Tooles have been a part of the reality TV franchise since the dawn of time. Of which we know, Joe’s dad Billy O’Toole was involved with the Irish mafia and he left his wife and Joe’s mom Jessica Clare to take care of Joe and the other kids on her own.

Joe’s dad is no longer alive. The patriarch was found dead in his Staten Island home on 14 July 2020.

Amid the second season, his family was looking for answers to how he died. His death was announced on the season finale after his body was found.

Months later, and it is said, Joe is still struggling with the mysterious demise of his dad.

While Joe and his siblings were growing up, his father had been in and out of jail seven times.

Remembering those days, Joe has shared that it was hard to get to know his dad properly. A couple of years before his dad died, Joe had said he was starting over with his dad who he believed “can be the sweetest guy or the devil’s best friend.”
