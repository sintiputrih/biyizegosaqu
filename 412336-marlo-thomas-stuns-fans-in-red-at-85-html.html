What Did Marlo Say about Fashion and Her Husband's Take On It?

In January 2017, Thomas was close to turning 80 and was launching a clothing line aptly called "That Woman" when she was asked why she felt it was important to still feel sexy at that age. The star said it was always important to feel that way as women and men had to be authentic.

Speaking about her husband's fashion, she revealed he would often wear jeans, V-neck cashmere sweaters, and striped shirts, and he looked good in them even if he could've worn the outfits 40 years before. She thought the most important is one's spiritâ€”which she dressed forâ€”and not her age.

The celebrity also described Donahue's style as preppy and conservative, while she leans more on classic. Thomas also shared that she consulted him on her clothing line, and while she admits, he's "not a fashionista," he would offer an opinion or two. For instance, he loved the blue dress she wore to her launch party and the suede jacket she featured in her collection.

Sharing more about her husband, the actress described him as cool and not critical, adding, " He gets a kick out of watching me enjoy what I do." When she told him about her idea for a clothing line, he thought it was great and supported her. "You're born to do this!" he egged.

When it came to how she and Donahue managed to make their marriage a success, the star says giving it time is what works. The couple would take trips alone, and since their first anniversary, they decided not to buy each other gifts but to spend their money on trips.

Speaking about age, Thomas said she didn't think women over 40 didn't want to look older but that they wished to appear as the girls inside them. During a conversation with her husband, they figured men brought some of their boyishness in them when they were older, while women did the same.

Marlo's Role as Ann Marie and Her Other Projects

In 2019, Thomas' role as Ann Marie in the series "That Girl" was dissected by "That Book About That Girl" author Stephen Cole. Cole said Thomas' character stood out because of her independence and aspirations as a woman.

Cole also revealed part of the appeal of the show was being able to meld fantasy and reality in Thomas' character. He recalled an episode where Ann Marie had no money and made tomato soup using ketchup, but the character still looked like she had stepped out of a fashion magazine.

"That Girl" ran from 1966 until 1971, and on September 8, 2023, the actress celebrated 57 years since the series was released with a throwback photo of herself in the role.

Thomas has kept herself busy through the years. In 2021, she revealed that she had a script to read, a book to write, and a podcast to record. She also had a Williams-Sonoma collection of entertaining essentials coming out, was tackling gun control laws and women's rights, and working with St. Jude's Children's Hospital.
