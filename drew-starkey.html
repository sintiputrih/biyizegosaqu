Drew Starkey, age 26, is an American actor who is known for his works in the TV series like Outer Banks and Scream. He commenced his professional career in 2014.

Drew’s Bio & Family

Drew Starkey was born to his parents, Todd Starkey and Jodi Ballard Hutto on June 3, 1993, in North Carolina, the United States. He shares a sibling bond with his brother, Logan Starkey, and his two sisters, Brooke Starkey and Mackayla Starkey. Drew’s parents parted ways with a divorce after which his mother married Brian Hutto.

For his education, Drew attended St. Stephens High School. Eventually, he enrolled in Western Carolina University in Cullowhee, North Carolina.  He graduated in 2016 with a double major in English and Theatre performance. Drew holds white ethnicity and his birth sign is Gemini.

Career & Net Worth

Drew began his acting endeavors with the 2014 short movie, The Radical Nortion of Gene Mutation. He later appeared his other short movies like Lost Soles (2015) and Up the Hill (2016). His major movie breaks are Love, Simon (2018), The Hate U Give (2018), and Just Mercy (2019).

On top of that, Drew ringed his television debut with the series, Mercy Street, in 2017. He then appeared on Ozark (2017), Doom Patrol (2019), and Scream (2019). His recent performance was as Rafe in the 2020 Netflix adventure-mystery series, Outer Banks.
