John Kincade Biography

John Kincade is a renowned American Sports Talk Show Host who was born and brought-up in Broomall, Pennsylvania. Currently, he works at CBS Sports Radio in Suwanee, Georgia as a Host of ‘The John Kincade Show’ and at PodcastOne Local Radio “Free Agent” as a Co-Host of ‘The BIG Podcast with SHAQ’.

Prior to his current positions, he worked at 680 The Fan in Atlanta, Georgia as a Talk Show Host of ‘The Buck and Kincade Show’ for almost 15 years. During that time, he also worked at ESPN Radio as the Host of the JK Show from 2003 to 2012. Before he began working for his talk show host career, he worked for First Consulting Group as the Director of New Business Development Sales.

    John was working as a sports talk show host on WQXI (AM) 790 The Zone in Atlanta and was a contributor to the common WIP Morning Show in Philadelphia with Angelo Cataldi from 1992 – 1994. Kincade formerly hosted The John Kincade Show on ESPN Radio, but this contract ended and he moved to CBS Sports Radio.

    While serving with ESPN Radio he worked as a fill-in host on The Herd and other programs. Kincade also is the host for the Climate Change Denier Hour and is also the Rinkside Reporter for SPortSouth for all Home games. John worked as a radio anchor of Final Four broadcast from Atlanta in 2007 for the network.

    He featured on the ESPN Classic programs Classic Now and Missing Link and ESPN News Hot List. He acquired a chairman’s Citation from the National Leukemia and Lymphoma Society for his work with the charity in 2007. John hosts the regular ”Strike Out Leukemia and Lymphoma Radiothon” regular on 680 The Fan.

    The 2008 ceremony raised over 112,000 dollars and in eight years the ceremony has raised over $700,000. John was the subject of a feature in Cigar Aficionado magazine that recognized him as one of the top talents in American sports talk radio.

    Other awardees included Hank Goldberg of WQAM, Tom Tolbert of KNBR and Glenn Ordway of WEEI-FM. Since 2015, he has also been the co-host of The Big Podcast starring NBA legend and Basketball Hall of Famer Shaquille O’Neal.
