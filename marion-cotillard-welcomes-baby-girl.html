No further details have been given. Marion, 41, and her actor-director partner Guillaume Canet, 43, are already parents to a five-year-old son named Marcel. She announced her second pregnancy on Instagram in September, writing: "Many years ago, I met the man of my life, father of our son and of the baby we are expecting." She added: "He is my love, my best friend, the only one I need."
