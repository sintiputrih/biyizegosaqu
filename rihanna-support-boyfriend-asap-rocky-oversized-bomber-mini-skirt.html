See Rihanna Support A$AP Rocky in an Oversized Bomber and Mini Skirt

 Posted on February 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  321 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Rihanna stepped out last night to cheer on A$AP Rocky at his latest perfomance.

The "Work" singer joined her partner at Red Studio in Los Angeles Thursday night, where the "D.M.B." rapper performed unreleased songs from his upcoming album Don't Be Dumb during his Amazon Music Live set. For the event, the Fenty mogul looked cool in an all-brown look that called back to the trends of '90s hip hop.

She wore a plain brown top and a snakeskin-print mini skirt under an oversized leather bomber jacket by Prada, which featured a black lining, an extra-large collar trimmed with peach fur, and a triangle logo pocket on the sleeve. She completed the look with an array of gold jewelry, including chunky gold hoops and thin chain necklaces, while carrying a matching brown top-handle bag.

The Y2K inspiration also continued through to the pop star's beauty look, with the star wearing her hair up in a braided bun with one long curtain bang covering part of her face.

Meanwhile, Rocky performed in an all-black outfit, layering his own leather bomber over a button-up denim shirt.

Though it's been less than a year since the couple welcomed their first child together last May, the new parents may already be looking forward to making their baby boy a big brother.

"Rihanna and A$AP are so in love and have the best time together," an insider told Entertainment Tonight. "They are also a great team when it comes to being parents to their baby boy. Rihanna always dreamed of being a mom and she takes a lot of pride in it. She would love to have more children with A$AP in the future."
