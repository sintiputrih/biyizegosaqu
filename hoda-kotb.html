Who is Today’s Co-Host, Hoda Kotb?

Hoda Kotb is a journalist and television personality famous as the co-host of NBC‘s Today show. Hoda is a successful woman and one of the foremost well-known faces in journalism.

Age, Height, Birthday, Parent

Kotb was born in 1964 to Egyptian parents, A.K. Kotb and Samaha Kotb, in Norman, Oklahoma, in the United States. She celebrates her birthday on the 9th of August. By 2023, she is 59 years old.

Her father was a fossil energy specialist who died at 51, and her mother, Samaha Kotb, works at the Library of Congress. She is the middle child of the Kotb family, with a brother named Adel and a sister named Hala. Hoda stands 5 feet 9 inches tall. She has managed to please a broad audience through her work.

Personal Life

In 2005, Kotb married a former tennis coach at the University of New Orleans, Burzis Kanga. But her marriage ended up in the year 2008. She disclosed her relationship with financier Joel Schiffman in 2013.

On February 21, 2017, Kotb announced that she had adopted a baby girl named Haley Joy Kotb after a battle with breast cancer that left her unable to conceive. Later, she announced that she was adopting another baby girl named Hope Catherine Kotb.

Career

Hoda started her journalism career as a news assistant for CBS News in 1986. She has also served as a correspondent for the network’s show Dateline NBC since 1998.

Kotb has released several books regarding her writing career, including Where They Belong: The Best Decisions People Almost Never Made, I Really Needed This Today, This Speaks to Me, and many more.
