Caroline Manning Biography and Wiki

Caroline Manning is an accomplished American Singer, Songwriter and Social Media Star. She is widely known for uploading videos and pictures with different variety of contents including vlogs, songs, makeup tutorials, pranks among other things on her Youtube Channel.

She mostly posts her contents more on YouTube and on Instagram from which she has amassed over 500K subscribers and followers. She posts cover songs as well as her own original songs which include ‘The Word of God’, ‘Cali Girl’, Dear Teachers at my school’ and ‘Dear People/Boys at my School’.

She was once invited by the renowned American singer Larry Gatlin in one of his shows which as well earned gain fame. She has a sister called Catherine who is featured in most of her YouTube videos, one of them is titled “sister reacts to song I wrote for her” which had over 10 million viewers. Her parents has also appeared.
