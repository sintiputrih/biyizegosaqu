Meghan Markle Friends - Who Are Meghan Markle's Friends?

 Posted on May 2, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  811 words  |  Martina Birk

Prior to their engagement announcement, Meghan Markle showed her commitment to Prince Harry by quitting her job on Suits and moving to England. But Markle's friends are scattered around the world, and despite now being based in the United Kingdom, she'll be traveling frequently for work in her new royal role. Here are just some of Markle's closest friends (and some possible bridesmaids).

Janina Gavankar

In an Instagram post from last year, Markle wrote of Janina Gavankar, "Oh how I love seeing my long time friend @janina... I just did the math—did we become friends 15 years ago?!? Whoa." With a friendship going back more than a decade, it's safe to say these two are close. Gavankar is also an actress who has appeared in shows such as True Blood and The Vampire Diaries.

Roland Mouret

Of her first meeting with fashion designer Roland Mouret, Markle told Fashion Magazine in April 2016, "I was in an hotel elevator in Istanbul, and this man in a bathrobe said, 'I love to dress you. I thought, 'Whaaat?' Then he told me who he was and we’ve been friends ever since." The pair are often photographed together, and Markle is a fan of Mouret's designs.

Serena Williams

Williams and Markle hit it off when they met at a celebrity charity football event in 2014, according to the Daily Mail. Markle wrote about becoming friends with the tennis superstar for her now-defunct blog The Tig:

"She quickly became a confidante I would text when I was traveling, the friend I would rally around for her tennis matches, and the down-to-earth chick I was able to grab lunch with a couple of weeks ago in Toronto. We are both the same age, have a penchant for hot sauces and adore fashion, but what connects us more than those things is perhaps our belief in exceeding expectations—our endless ambition."

Priyanka Chopra

Quantico star Priyanka Chopra met Markle at ELLE's Women in Television dinner in 2016. Chopra famously corrected Wendy Williams for describing Markle as "Prince Harry's girlfriend." Her retort: "Meghan Markle, actress. Suits. Her achievements... just saying."

It's already being speculated that Chopra will serve as one of Markle's bridesmaids.

Markus Anderson

It was previously reported that Markus Anderson, the Soho House Group consultant and alleged mutual friend of the couple, had introduced Prince Harry and Markle. However, as the soon-to-be married couple revealed in their engagement interview on the BBC, they were set up on a blind date by a female mutual friend.

However, Anderson is extremely close to Markle, and has traveled all over the world with her. He's often featured on her Instagram account, and was even photographed sitting next to her at the Invictus Games this year.

Misha Nonoo

According to the Daily Mail, Markle's friend, fashion designer Misha Nonoo most likely introduced the Suits actor to Prince Harry. Her estranged husband apparently went to Eton with Prince Harry and Prince William, so Nonoo definitely has the connections to have set up a blind date between the now-happy couple.

Abigail Spencer

Fellow actor Abigail Spencer recently spoke to Vanity Fair about Markle's relationship with Prince Harry and said, "It’s just wonderful to see her so in love."

Lindsay Roth

According to the Daily Mail, Lindsay Roth hit it off with Markle during a Toni Morrison literature class at Northwestern University. The pair are definitely close as Markle served as maid of honor at Roth's wedding in August 2016. Will Roth return the favor in spring 2018?

Jessica Mulroney

Jessica Mulroney is a friend who could come in very handy. She's a fashion and bridal stylist, which Markle will need very soon. According to Town and Country, Mulroney dresses Justin Trudeau's wife, Sophie Gregoire-Trudeau, who is also friendly with Markle.

Mulroney and Markle reportedly bonded over yoga when Markle moved to Canada to work on Suits, Harper's Bazaar UK reports.

Sarah Rafferty and Gina Torres from Suits

In August 2016, Markle posted on Instagram about Rafferty and Torres and said, "Celebrating our friendship of six years and counting. So much deep love for these two women." After seven seasons of Suits, it seems likely that Markle is still in touch with her co-stars, and that many of them will be invited to the royal wedding.

Olivia Palermo

When asked by Good Housekeeping who her style crush was in May 2017, Markle said, "my friend Olivia Palermo—I mean, she's everyone's style crush." She also cited Gwyneth Paltrow and Miranda Kerr as fashion influences, but she called Palermo her friend, and the pair are often photographed together. It's very possible that Palermo will put in an appearance at the royal wedding.
