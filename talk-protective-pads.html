What About Status Moves?

So does this mean that protective pads don't protect from status moves like taunt and encore? And when u mean contact moves does that include physical move's effects like Fake Out causing 100% flinch chance? HappyDuckJJ (talk) 20:47, 19 February 2020 (UTC)

There are no status moves that make contact. The Fake Out example is incorrect. Protect Pads have no effect on the secondary effects of moves. However, if a Pokémon holding Protective Pads uses Fake Out on a Pokémon with Gooey, Gooey will not decrease that Pokémon's Speed. --SnorlaxMonster 11:32, 20 February 2020 (UTC)

Ooh ok thank you. I misread what the wiki page said. HappyDuckJJ (talk) 12:13, 20 February 2020 (UTC)
