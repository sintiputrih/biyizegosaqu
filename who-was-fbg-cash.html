A MEMBER of the Fly Boy Gang, FBG Cash was a Chicago-based rapper.

He first rose to fame in the 2010s with his popular drill-style beats.

Who was FBG Cash?

From the South Side of Chicago, Illinois, FBG Cash was a drill rapper.

He started his career with the name Cashout063, also going by just Cash.

The musician was from the “O Block” in Chicago, which spans from around 63rd street to 65th street and is known as one of the most dangerous places in the city.

Many popular rappers come from this neighborhood, including Chief Keef, King Von, and ARoy.

What happened to FBG Cash?

On Friday, June 10, 2022, FBG Cash was reportedly killed in a shooting in Chicago, per ABC7.

A woman was also reportedly wounded in the incident.

According to the Chicago Police Department, two people were shot around 5.32am CT on the 1600 block of West 81st Street.

FBG Cash and the woman were reportedly inside a vehicle when another car pulled up and an unknown offender got out and opened fire.

What is the Fly Boy Gang?

Fly Boy Gang is a Chicago-based rap group.

Members over the years have included: FBG Dutchie, FBG Young, FBG Bigga, FBG Brick, FBG Butta, FBG Cash, FBG Duck, FBG Kels, FBG Meezle, FBG WhiteBoa, and FYB J Mane.

In 2020, FBG Duck was shot and killed in Chicago’s Gold Coast neighborhood.

He was 26 years old at the time.
