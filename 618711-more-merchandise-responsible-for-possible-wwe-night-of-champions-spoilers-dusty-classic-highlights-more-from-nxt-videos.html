Possible WWE Night Of Champions Spoilers?

According to the Wrestling Observer Newsletter, WWE is prepared to re-release John Cena merchandise that features the United States title on it, possibly hinting at Cena winning the title back at WWE Night Of Champions. 

One would assume it is leftover “Unites States Champ Is Here” shirts, which are actually still listed live on WWEShop.com, or it is just a coincidence. There was also concern of Summerslam spoilers after WWE released John Cena “15X” merchandise so close to the event; in this case however, it would appear to be previously produced merchandise, not new prints like last month with Cena.
