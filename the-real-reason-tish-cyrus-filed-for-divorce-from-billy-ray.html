Miley Cyrus' mom, Tish recently filed for divorce from Billy Ray Cyrus... And it's actually not the first time. The couple has had a lot of troubles during their 30-year marriage. How Tish & Billy Ray Cyrus Met

The couple met in 1991 after Billy Ray's divorce from his first wife, Cindy Smith whom he married in 1986. In 1992, Tish got pregnant with their first child, Miley. But at the time, Billy Ray was already expecting a child with Kristin Luckey. The latter gave birth to a baby boy named Christopher in April that year. Miley arrived in November. The Achy Breaky Heart singer had to pick which of the women he would marry. He eventually chose Tish. They tied the knot in their living room in Franklin, Tennessee in 1993.

Billy Ray's record label advised against the wedding due to his popularity with female fans."This train may come off the tracks, but I'm going to be a dad," the musician said of his decision to marry Tish during an interview with ABC in 2004. "I'm going to be a husband, and try to have something in my life that is right." He also adopted her children from her first marriage: Brandi and Trace Cyrus. In 1994, the couple welcomed their second child together, a son named Braison. Their youngest child, Noah Cyrus arrived later in 2000.
