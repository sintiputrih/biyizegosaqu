Ferguson FE-35 Utility Tractor was equipped with a Standard 23C diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which delivers 34 hp (25.4 kW) horsepower.

Ferguson FE-35 Compact Utility Tractor featured with a Standard 2.2L 4-cyl gasoline and Standard 2.3L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, differential shoe brakes, 6-speed unsynchronized gear transmission, 3093 lbs weight, 6 forward and 2 reverse gears.
