Wiki/Biography

Palak Tiwari was born on Sunday, 8 October 2000 (age 21 years; as of 2021) in Mumbai. Her zodiac sign is Libra.

Palak Tiwari in childhood with her parents

Palak enjoyed dancing since childhood and was inclined towards becoming an actress from a very young age. She loved watching actresses dance onscreen when she was in her teens. She was equally good at studies in her school days and secured 83% in her class 10 board examination. Palak attained a B.A. in Psychology from Mumbai’s Mithibai College. [1]

Parents & Siblings

Palak Tiwari is the daughter of the television actress Shweta Tiwari from Shweta’s first marriage with Raja Chaudhary. After Shweta divorced Raja, she took custody of Palak.

Career

In 2017, Tiwari made her acting debut with the Bollywood film “Quickie.” In 2020, she bagged a role in the film “Rosie: The Saffron Chapter.”

In October 2021, she featured in Harrdy Sandhu‘s video song ‘Bijlee Bijlee.’
