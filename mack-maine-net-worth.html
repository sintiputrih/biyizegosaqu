Mack Maine: Career

Although Mack Maine began rapping at the age of 11, he did not have a smooth career like other successful musicians.

Maine made his notable music career appearance in his close friend Lil Wayne’s third single entitled Got Money.

Mack Maine earned his first-ever career award, at the 2008 Ozone Award, Patiently Waiting: Louisiana award.

Subsequently, Maine and Wayne collaborated for several years and released numerous singles, including Throw It Back, Celebrate, and many more.

In the meantime, the American rapper Maine dropped several mixtapes, including Billionaire Minds (2011), Don’t Let It Go Waste (2012), and others.

Finally, he dropped his debut studio album entitled We Are Young Money on December 21, 2009.

The album peaked top spot on the US Top Rap Albums chart.

Following the successful debut album, Maine released his second album in 2013, entitled Rich Gang.

This album received the second spot in both Top R&B/Hip Hop Album as well as Top Rap Album charts in the United States.

The following year, the star rap artist Maine dropped his third album entitled Young Money: Rise of an Empire on March 11, 2014.

To sum up, he has released three singles as a lead singer, whereas numerous tracks as a featured artist for many top-notch musicians.
