Cardi B only gets more sensational each year. Now that she’s 31, let’s take a look at her hottest outfits of all time.

Cardi B has become an undeniable icon since she burst onto the music scene in 2017. The rapper shot to superstardom with her very first single and has been a staple at award shows and high-profile events ever since. She’s given dozens of buzzed about television performances and has walked every red carpet from the Grammys to the Met Gala.

In just a few years, Cardi has already become one to watch on the fashion scene, and fans and critics alike are always dying to see what look she pulls out next. While many of her looks are quite eclectic, Cardi is also known for her super sexy style. She loves showing off her bombshell curves in whatever outfit fits the moment.
