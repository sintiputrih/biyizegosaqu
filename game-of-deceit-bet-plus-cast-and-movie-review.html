Meet The Cast of Game Of Deceit On BET Plus

Game Of Deceit BET Plus cast are Denise Boutte, Kevin Savage, Melissa L Williams and Dawn Noel.

Denise Boutte As Maya

Game of Deceit actress Denise is an American actress and model. She was born on January 19, 1982, in the town of Maurice in the state of Louisiana, United States.

She joined Louisiana State University to study Microbiology, doing Pre-Med as her major but she graduated with a degree in Mass Communications with a minor in Marketing which was not expected.

Denise was invited into the film industry when she was discovered by a talent agency.

Since then, she has acted in many movies and series such as Way of Vampire, Everybody Hates Chris, Girlfriends, Sister’s Keeper, Days of Our Lives, Why Did I Get Married? and Key and Peele.

Denise is currently married to her husband Kevin Boutte, since 2003 and has a daughter named Jordan.

She is also the CEO of Weeziana Girl Culinary which provides varieties of seasoning powder.

Kevin Savage As Ian

Kevin Savage plays the role of Ian in the movie Game Of Deceit where he abuses his wife Maya and she can’t do anything about it.

Kevin is an actor and artist who has been working in BET movies for a long time. Savage is also a very health-conscious individual and likes to keep himself healthy.

His first movie as an actor was called Computer Love where he played the role of Gene.

Since then, he has worked in many movies and series. He is best known for his roles in movies and series such as The Quad, The Passage, Lovecraft Country and Single Black’ Female.

Kevin’s other work includes Underground Kings, Chasing Waterfalls, Trashville, Secret Society, A Wesley Christmas and Hush.

Melissa L Williams As Elle

Actress Melissa plays the role of Elle in the movie. Melissa is an American actress and model who grew up in Oklahoma City with her six siblings. She celebrates her birthday on August 31.

She graduated from Clark Atlanta University with a degree in Bachelor in Fine Arts. She also worked in many productions at her university ranging from classical ones to musicals.

She began her first role in the tv series The Game where she played the role of Jasmine in 2015.

Dawn Noel As Liz

Dawn plays the role of Liz in the movie Game of Deceit she sides with her police partner Ian.

Dawn was born on December 27, 1976, and was raised and comes from the busy city of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States.

Actress Dawn graduated high school from Creative and Performing Arts High School where she majored in Dance.

Though the actress started her career in acting a short movie called Trust Love, it did not work much in her movie credit.

Only in Glee in 2009, where she acted as Jayelle, did her journey as an actress become stable.

Since then Dawn has acted in movies and series such as My Normal, Salsa Lessons, NCIS, Grand Slammed, Sista Did What?, 22 Years, La Golda, White People Money and No Other Way.

Game Of Deceit Movie Review and Rating

BET Plus new thriller drama movie is about a woman named Maya who is now abused by her husband using sleeping drugs.

She wants to go to the police to ask for help but even then she can’t because her husband herself is a police officer and he is abusing his authority.

She can’t ask for anyone’s help nor she can escape from her husband Ian.

Now, her only option is to collect all the evidence against her husband and provide the evidence to someone who can take down her husband.

The movie has not been rated till now. So, the viewers will have to wait for the rating of the movie. But there are some quite good reviews for the movie.

In the comment section of the movie’s trailer on YouTube, the comments mention that the movie is nice, while the other comment mentions that the actor of the movie is talented.

On Twitter of BET Plus, the viewers commented that the movie is good.

One of the accounts also commented that they are going to watch the movie and other commented that they want to know the thought process behind the movie.
