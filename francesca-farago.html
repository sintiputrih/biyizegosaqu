Wiki/Biography

Francesca Farago was born on Friday, 18 November 1993 (age 26 years; as in 2019) in British Columbia, Canada. Her zodiac sign is Saggitarius.

Francesca Farago As a Child

She did her graduation from Carleton University Law School in Ottawa, Canada. She began her career as a social media influencer. Soon, she started getting modelling assignments; working with brands like Misha Swim, Revolve, and Fashion Nova.
