15 Facts About Double Decker Arts Festival

 Posted on February 8, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  150 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Double Decker Arts Festival is an annual event that takes place in Oxford, Mississippi, attracting thousands of visitors from all over the country. This popular festival, named after the iconic double-decker bus that roams the streets of Oxford, offers a unique blend of art, music, food, and cultural experiences. From its humble beginnings in 1996, the Double Decker Arts Festival has grown into a highly anticipated event that showcases the vibrant arts scene of Oxford and celebrates the rich heritage of the community. With a wide array of activities, performances, and exhibits, this festival has something for everyone to enjoy.
