These days, Addison is moving toward acting in addition to her TikTok branding, which likely means she'll continue to rake in the cash. The real question is, though: how is Addison Rae spending her net worth?

How Is Addison Rae Making Money?

To clarify her impressive net worth, sources say that Addison Rae amassed her $5 million in only a year, because of her endorsement deals plus merchandise. Fans of the TikToker have seen her rock Addison Rae-branded outfits, and there are collabs with Reebok, Hollister, and more to add up.

What that means for her income is clear, but what it means for her spending isn't quite. After all, as an influencer, Addison Rae is probably posting up with a lot of free products. Still, there are a few things she clearly indulges in and spends her own hard-earned cash on.

Does Addison Rae Own A Mansion?

The first question about Addison Rae's net worth is whether she's invested some of it in a million-dollar home. Apparently, she has. Easterling reportedly bought a $3M home in Los Angeles sometime in 2019.

Her not-so-humble abode has six bedrooms, seven and a half baths, and has a pool plus amazing views of the tree-covered neighborhood. Fans can spot various features of the home in Addison's TikTok videos plus her Instagram posts.

Addison Also Shells Out For Her Pets

Sure, her mansion is one way Addison is investing her cash. But she has a few other expenses to consider, too. For one thing, there's her Frenchie Bulldog, whose name is Maui.

Addison posts a lot of pup content, but fans don't always get to see her dog. In fact, Easterling seems to post with a handful of different dog breeds, and there's no telling exactly who they belong to.

But in one interview, Addison talked about gifts she was giving her pup for the holidays, so it's clear she has at least one dog that she loves to spoil.

What Kind Of Car Does Addison Rae Drive?

Some celebrities like to collect mansions, some collect bling, but what does Addison Rae like? Turns out, a hot pink Tesla Model X. At least, it used to be hot pink.

While the car was ostensibly a gift from her dad, Addison only drove the pink car for a short time before completely redoing it. She explained that it drew too much attention, especially at times when she just wanted to, say, hit up the drive-thru.

Addison Rae Does Travel...

Fans can assume that when Addison Rae is working on, say, films like the upcoming 'He's All That,' she's getting paid for her flight time. But other times, Addison is likely footing her own bill for expenses.

Luckily, a lot of her picturesque sites for social media snaps appear to be local, but Addison doesn't always stick close to home. But anyone can tell the TikToker that travel is a good investment, so there's no point in stressing over spending a bit of cash on it (for the experience!).

Addison Reinvests In Her Business

Though she could probably live on her existing millions for a while, Addison is more business-minded than that. She's currently pushing her beauty brand ITEM Beauty, which is available at Sephora, so she's clearly put some cash flow into her business.

Though having BFF and "mentor" Kourtney Kardashian repping for her probably helps, there's always money that needs to go into a new startup, and Addison likely worked hard to get the ball rolling. There's also the influencer's clothing line, which is bringing in income now, but had to get started somehow.
