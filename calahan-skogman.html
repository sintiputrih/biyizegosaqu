Wiki/Biography

Calahan Skogman was born on May 13, 1993 (age 28 years; as of 2021) in Green Bay, Wisconsin, the United States. His zodiac sign is Taurus. After completing his schooling at Seymour High School, Skogman attended the University of Wisconsin where he played basketball for the university team. Later, he went to the University of Southern California to pursue a degree in Master’s of Fine Arts in acting.

Career

During his time at the University of Southern California, Calahan acted in many plays such as Julius Caesar, Swimmers, Children of the Sun, Eurydice, The Metal Children, Catch Me if You Can etc. He made his stage debut with the short drama ‘G. I. Jose’ in 2017. In 2018, he played the lead role in the movie ‘Smile.’ In 2019, he worked in the supporting role for the movie “Blood Puppet! Christmas ’94” (2019). In 2020, Calahan got the opportunity to work in the Netflix series ‘Shadow and Bone’ (2021).
