The series features a band of hardcore hunters, who take it upon themselves to bring light to the mysterious sightings. Jacob Lowe is one of those hunters, who has been at it for a long time. Wiki/Bio Facts

A life of secrecy

Jacob Lowe, also known by the name Jake is a man of secrets. He has revealed very little about himself and is not too keen on sharing. As a result, media outlets have been able to glean little to no information from the man. What makes this even more difficult is that Jacob Lowe doesn’t like social media. Life on Mountain Monsters

Jacob Lowe went from a seemingly normal person to a reality star in a span of a few days. While Jacob had one or two acting credits to his name, it was nothing compared to Mountain Monsters. Jacob is one of the original six hunters to feature in the series. A part of Appalachian Investigators of Mysterious Sightings (AIMS), Jacob is often the first one charging into combat. There is not much that can faze Jacob. His actions on the set have earned Jacob the nickname of “Buck”. Similar to the animal his name represents, Jacob is headstrong and is ready to put on a shift.

Jacob joined the cast of Mountain Monsters during the first seasons in 2013. The reality TV star has been consistent and has been a big part of every season until now.

The eighth season of Mountain Monsters concluded on March 13, 2022, and judging by the reception from the fans one more season is likely to follow. Whatever the future may hold, Jacob has already proven to be an important part of the show. He has appeared in 80 episodes from 2013 to 2022.

One of the most recognizable features of Jacob, other than his headstrong nature is his size. The man weighed quite a lot and had to face several health problems due to his size. Over the years, Jacob has worked to get his weight under control. He has lost a considerable amount of weight and the results are for all to see.

For the longest time, Jacob kept his fans at bay, but as things started to get out of hand, the reality TV star had no option but to come forward.
