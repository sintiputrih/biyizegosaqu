Joe Jonas Celebrates Sophie Turner's &quot;Two Moods&quot; in Birthday Post

 Posted on March 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  330 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Joe Jonas loves his wife, Sophie Turner—no matter her mood.

The Jonas Brother took to Instagram yesterday to share a hilarious birthday tribute to his spouse who turned 25. In the post, Jonas shares two contrasting photos of Turner: one of her glammed up in a monochromatic outfit composed of a sweater and matching cream-colored trousers by MVP Wardrobe, platform boots, and an oat-hued overcoat, with retro white sunglasses; and another of her embracing the effects of quarantine in a bright blue hoodie scrunched up over her head, a messy 'do, and a makeup-free face.

"Happy Birthday babe. You have two moods and I love them both equally 💜 love you @sophiet," wrote Jonas.

Turner also turned to social media to bask in the birthday love. The Game of Thrones star shared a slew of birthday messages to her Instagram Story from friends and family, and also shared a sneak peek into how she was celebrating her special day at home with Jonas and their daughter, Willa Jonas.

The actress, sporting an oversized white knit sweater and her signature sleek strands, posed under a bouquet of balloons, and in front of two giant gold number balloons—a 2 and a 5, of course—and thanked friends and fans for sending her well wishes.

"Thank you for the birthday wishes," wrote Turner alongside the image and a sweet GIF that read, "To the moon and back." The actress concluded the post with the quirky message, "25-ing and thriving."

Sophie Turner//Instagram

Jonas celebrated his wife just a week prior for Valentine's Day, when he shared a sweet throwback post of Turner while she was still pregnant with their daughter.

"Happy Valentine's Day to my 2 Valentines ❤️ throwback," he wrote.
