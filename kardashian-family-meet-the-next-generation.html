Kardashian-Jenner Family's Next Generation: Photos

 Posted on January 17, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  150 words  |  Larita Shotwell

While making their mark on the entertainment and business world, the Kardashian-Jenner clan is also raising their next generation.

Kourtney Kardashian was the first of Kris Jenner’s children to have kids of her own. Kourtney and her now-ex Scott Disick welcomed their eldest son, Mason, in December 2009, followed by daughter Penelope three years later.

Kim Kardashian was the next sibling to expand her family, giving birth to her and Kanye West’s daughter North in June 2013. In 2015, Kim and Kourtney had sons, Saint and Reign, respectively.

Kim’s next two children with West, whom she divorced in 2021, arrived via surrogate. Chicago was born in January 2018 and Psalm in May 2019.
