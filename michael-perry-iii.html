All About Avriel Hooks Husband Michael Perry III

Michael Perry III (age 21, born in 2002) is a Lynchburg man who found himself at the center of a heart-wrenching tragedy. He is now facing murder charges related to the untimely death of his wife, Avriel Hooks.

Avriel Hooks, Michael’s wife, went missing on August 22, 2023. After a week of frantic searching, her lifeless body was discovered on a Tuesday. Her remains were found in a wooded area off U.S. 60 (Lexington Turnpike), Amherst, VA.

In the wake of Avriel’s disappearance, Michael Perry III was taken into custody at the age of 21 and charged with second-degree murder as well as the unlawful disposal of a dead body. He was swiftly transported to the Blue Ridge Regional Jail, where he is presently being held without the option of posting bail.

Michael Facebook Posts

Amidst the search for Avriel, Michael turned to social media for support. On his Facebook page, he shared his distress, writing,

“My wife is missing. I’m not okay, yo. The police questioned me for 5 hours and held me for 12. I need any information you can give. I’m a husband and a father. She’s my wife and the mother of my child. We miss her.”

As the days went by without any resolution, Michael’s anguish deepened. He posted once more on August 25, pouring out his heart on social media:

“If you know how much I love my wife and my son, you would understand how hurt I am. I’m constantly searching and looking everywhere for my wife. I’m not okay. This is the mother of my child. I’m lost.”

Michael Perry and Avriel Hooks’s Married Life

Michael and Avriel got married on November 4, 2022. They were excited about their life together and shared lots of happy pictures on social media.

Their joy grew when their baby boy, Michael Garnett Perry IV, was born on March 21, 2023. Michael proudly told everyone about his son’s birth on Facebook, mentioning his weight, height, and how healthy he was.

Things seemed good, but then something changed. Around July 31, Michael stopped posting pictures of Avriel and their kids. It looked like something was wrong. Avriel even updated her Facebook status to “Separated,” showing that they weren’t together anymore. It’s sad when love takes a different path.

And then came the unthinkable. Just a few months after they separated, Michael was arrested for killing Avriel, the person he once loved. It’s really hard to understand how things can change so much.
