Net Worth Of Marilyn Manson

Marilyn Manson started his career with his band, Marilyn Manson & the Spooky Kids in 1989 and their debut album, Portrait of an American Family became hit after it's release in 1994. Some of their EPs and albums are Smells Like Children, Antichrsit Superstar, Mechanical Animals, Holy Wood, The Golden Age of Grotesque, The High End of Low, Born Villain, The Pale Emperor, and more.
