Surprise! Blake Lively Is Pregnant With Her and Ryan Reynolds' Fourth Child

By Michelle Stein

Sep. 15 2022, Updated 2:53 p.m. ET

Just one day after Ryan Reynolds published a video of his colonoscopy for the world to see, he and wife Blake Lively made a big announcement: They're expecting baby No. 4! Blake revealed her pregnancy news by showing up to a red carpet while sporting a bump, leaving fans wondering: When is her due date?

Keep reading to learn all about the reveal, the reactions, and when we might expect Ryan and Blake's fourth child.

Blake Lively revealed her fourth pregnancy in September 2022.

On Sept. 15, 2022, Blake showed up at the 10th Annual Forbes Power Women's Summit wearing a sequined mini dress with long sleeves,People reported. She also happened to be sporting a surprise bump! Neither she nor Ryan have commented yet on the suspected pregnancy.

When is Blake Lively's due date?

The famous duo are known for keeping their private life separate from the media, and even keep their three daughters out of the spotlight. Fans last saw the family all together when they were photographed at Ryan’s Hollywood Walk of Fame Ceremony in 2016.

However, if Blake is around three months pregnant (which is when most expecting mothers announce), fans can expect baby Reynolds to arrive in late March or early April 2023.

This isn't the first time she's debuted a bump on a red carpet.

Back in 2019, Blake turned heads wearing a skin-tight yellow Retrofete gown, which showed off her bump with baby No. 3. The expectant parents smiled for the cameras as Blake rocked her angles showcasing her small baby bump at the premiere of Ryan's movie Detective Pikachu.

The memes about Taylor Swift and Blake Lively's new baby are already rampant.

When fans heard Blake and Ryan would be welcoming a fourth child to their family, they took to social media to offer messages of congratulations.

Many also wondered whether Blake's BFF Taylor Swift would use her new baby's voice in her upcoming "Gorgeous (Taylor's Version)." Or, perhaps drop a name reveal in an upcoming song, as she has a habit of including the Reynolds kid's monikers in her work. (James, Betty, and Inez have all been featured.)

Some poked fun at the idea that Taylor and Blake were already collaborating on a name for the baby for a new song.

Others wondered if there would be revisions to Taylor's album "Folklore" since Blake and Ryan's three older daughters' names were highlighted in its songs.

Still others wondered if Taylor might opt for a more traditional baby gift this time around.

However Taylor does or doesn't end up incorporating the newest Reynolds child into her work, we can’t wait to meet baby No. 4. Congrats to the happy couple!
