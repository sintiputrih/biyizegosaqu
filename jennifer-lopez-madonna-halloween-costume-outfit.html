Jennifer Lopez's impeccable style is always inspiring, and it turns out that her Halloween costumes are every bit as chic as her everyday wardrobe.

Sharing several series of snaps on Instagram, J.Lo revealed that she dressed up as Madonna for Halloween, and the outfit was, quite frankly, flawless. Replicating the famous look that Madonna wore to the MTV VMAs in 1984, Lopez looked stunning in a lacy white dress, accessorized with fingerless gloves, star-shaped earrings, pearls, and a "boy toy" belt.
