Lopez vs Lopez Season Cast: Get ready for more Lopez shenanigans! The critically acclaimed sitcom “Lopez vs. Lopez” is back for its second season, bringing together old-school humor and modern family dynamics for a side-splitting ride. This heartwarming comedy, starring the real-life father-daughter duo of George Lopez and Mayan Lopez, is set to premiere on April 2, 2024, exclusively on NBC.

What is the Release Date of Lopez vs Lopez and Where to Watch?

Mark your calendars for Tuesday, April 2nd! Season 2 kicks off with back-to-back episodes at 8:00 PM and 8:30 PM Eastern Time. Get your laughter fix by tuning in to NBC or streaming the episodes live on the NBC website or app. Don’t miss out on the fun – if you haven’t already, subscribe to Hulu or Peacock, where you can catch up on Season 1 and future episodes whenever you crave a giggle.

What is the Lopez vs Lopez Season Cast?

The heart of the show lies in its quirky ensemble cast. Get ready to reacquaint yourself with these lovable characters:

  • George Lopez: The iconic comedian himself returns as George, Mayan’s old-school father with a heart of gold and a penchant for outdated advice.
  • Mayan Lopez: Bringing her relatable humor, Mayan navigates motherhood, romance, and career aspirations, often clashing with her traditional dad.
  • Matt Shively: Playing Quinten, Mayan’s supportive and slightly bewildered boyfriend, he tries to navigate the Lopez family dynamics with patience and good humor.
  • Selenis Leyva: Rosie, George’s sassy ex-wife, adds spice to the mix with her unfiltered opinions and fiery personality.
  • Brice Gonzalez: As Chance, Mayan’s adorable and curious son, he steals hearts with his innocent questions and hilarious reactions to the family’s antics.
  • Al Madrigal: Victor, Rosie’s witty and charming new boyfriend, brings a fresh perspective to the family dynamic.

Expect guest appearances throughout the season, including comedy legends like Cheech Marin and Rita Moreno. With this hilarious cast, every episode promises a laugh-out-loud experience.

What to Expect in Lopez vs Lopez Season 2?

The new season delves deeper into the relationships between the characters, exploring their personal journeys and growth. Get ready for:

  • Family dynamics on fire: George and Mayan continue to clash over parenting styles, leading to hilarious and relatable situations.
  • Romance with a twist: Mayan’s relationship with Quinten faces new challenges, while Rosie navigates the exciting (and sometimes awkward) world of dating.
  • Career aspirations: Both George and Mayan chase their individual dreams, facing setbacks and triumphs along the way.
  • Culture clashes: The show masterfully blends traditional and modern perspectives, sparking insightful conversations and humorous mishaps.
  • Special occasions: From holidays to family gatherings, every episode brings heartwarming moments that celebrate the importance of family.

Expect the trademark Lopez humor – witty puns, timely jokes, and relatable situations wrapped in a warm embrace of family love.
